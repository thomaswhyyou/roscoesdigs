Holidays

Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s walks are a flat $23. For all other holidays, standard rates apply. If you have the day off from work and do not need a visit (Memorial Day, Labor Day, etc.), 24 hour notice must be given or you may be charged Late Notice fee. Please make sure your apartment is well-stocked to handle all of your pets' needs.

Cancellations

Please cancel a scheduled walk in advance of 24 or more hours. $3.27 Late Notice fee will be charged if any walk or visit is not cancelled 24 hours ahead.

Payments

Payments are accepted by check, cash or via PayPal. $3.27 Late Payment fee will be charged if a given payment is paid more than 24 hours after the invoice due date.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use Roscoe's Digs?

Roscoe’s Digs guarantees that your pet will be receiving the most loving and attentive care possible. We do not lump pets together on walks or cage them up! We aim to provide you with peace of mind, and to give your pets a great time!

What will you need from me before we can get started?

We'll need a few forms filled out (such as contracts, vet release, pet profile), information on the services you'd like, and agreed method of accessing your apartment.

Are you bonded, insured and licensed?

Roscoe’s Digs is fully licensed and bonded, and we can provide documentation upon request.

Who are the pet sitters who will be coming into my home?

Roscoe’s Digs hires the most dedicated, experienced animal lovers. Each employee is professionally trained, and put through a background check for everyone’s security.

How do I make my house keys available to my pet sitter?

During the Meet-and-Greet session we can discuss a suitable method of accesing your apartment. Typically the pet owners give Roscoe’s Digs a set of keys. If you live in a doorman building you can choose to leave a set with the doorman for us.

Do you have references? Can I contact your clients for more details on what it's like to work with your company?

Any time! We have a plenty of references, current and previous. Request a few to get the feel of how we work!
